About Me

I am a current sophomore at Boise State University studying the GIMM (Gaming Interactive Mobile Media) program. I'm looking to become a game developer to bring my ideas and designs into the world. The purpose of this website is to display the skills and projects that I've developed in the last two years of college.

More about the program I'm in...

Scouts Adventure

Scouts Adventure is my first 2D platformer and video game. This project was my introduction to C#, Unity, Photoshop, and graphic design. It gave me a delightful opportunity to be introduced to game development. The most enjoyable part of the project was brainstorming and organizing the game concept.

Quantum Entangled

Quantum Entangled is a group project that tells the love story of shapes to simplify the complex concept of quantum entanglement. I was the main developer of the art and concept, not so much of the code. The most enjoyable part of the project was working with the team and getting to express ideas and work together to create something unique.

Building a Snake Enclosure

This 360 VR video is a simulation and educational video that teaches the user to build a snake enclosure. The most beneficial asset I learned from this artifact was VR development. The most enjoyable part of the project was working with Unity to create a VR experience. It was something foreign to me, but despite the challenge, I really enjoyed learning the wonders that VR holds.
